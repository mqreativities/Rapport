Rapport is a verified counselor discovery and connection platform. It does NOT host therapy sessions, video calls, messaging, or audio calls. It only connects clients to licensed counselors and collects a consultation access fee.

The platform must be clean, professional, trust-centered, and minimal. Currency must be displayed in Nigerian Naira (₦). Gender selection options must be limited to Male and Female only wherever required.

⸻

CORE PRODUCT STRUCTURE

Rapport has three major parts:
	1.	Client Marketplace (Find & Hire Counselors)
	2.	Problem-Based Communities (Subscription Model)
	3.	Digital Resource Marketplace (Like Selar)

There is also an Organization hiring interface.

⸻

IMPORTANT SYSTEM RULES
	•	Rapport does NOT host therapy sessions.
	•	No internal messaging or calls.
	•	After payment of consultation access fee, client receives counselor’s verified contact details.
	•	Counselors must upload valid professional license before approval.
	•	Admin must verify license before counselor profile goes live.
	•	Nigeria-focused MVP (Naira currency, Nigerian compliance context).

USER ROLES

CLIENT INTERFACE

Screens to Design:

	•	Sign Up / Login

	•	Client Dashboard
	•	Browse Counselors
	•	Counselor Profile Page
	•	Pay Consultation Access Fee Screen (₦)
	•	Payment Confirmation
	•	Access Counselor Contact Details
	•	My Communities
	•	My Purchased Resources
	•	Profile & Settings

⸻

Consultation Access Model

Client pays a small token (₦X) to unlock:
	•	Counselor’s phone number
	•	Email
	•	Office address (if available)
	•	Booking link (external)

Rapport does not process session payments.

⸻

COUNSELOR INTERFACE

Onboarding:
	•	Counselor Sign Up
	•	Upload Professional License (Mandatory)
	•	Upload Certification Documents
	•	Identity Verification
	•	Admin Approval Flow (Pending → Approved → Rejected)

Profile goes live only after verification.

⸻

Counselor Dashboard:
	•	Profile Management
	•	License Status Indicator (Verified Badge)
	•	Consultation Access Purchases (List of clients who unlocked profile)
	•	Community Management (if assigned as manager)
	•	Upload Digital Resources
	•	Set Resource Price (₦)
	•	Resource Sales Analytics
	•	Payout Settings (Bank details)

⸻

PROBLEM-BASED COMMUNITIES

Communities are categorized by specific issues, for example:
	•	Anxiety
	•	Relationship Issues
	•	Academic Stress
	•	Career Confusion
	•	Depression
	•	Workplace Burnout

Each community:
	•	Has assigned licensed counselor(s) as Community Manager(s)
	•	Requires monthly subscription (₦)
	•	Includes:
	•	Structured discussion threads
	•	Educational posts
	•	Live event announcements (external)
	•	Moderation tools

Clients subscribe monthly per community.

Rapport earns subscription revenue.

⸻

DIGITAL RESOURCE MARKETPLACE (Selar-Style)

Counselors can:
	•	Upload digital products (PDFs, courses, toolkits)
	•	Set price in ₦
	•	Add description
	•	Upload preview
	•	Track sales

Clients can:
	•	Browse resources
	•	Purchase securely
	•	Download after payment

Rapport earns commission on digital product sales.

⸻

ORGANIZATION INTERFACE (HIRING ONLY)

Organizations use Rapport to:
	•	Create Company Account
	•	Upload Company Details
	•	Browse Verified Counselors
	•	Filter by specialization
	•	Pay Hiring Access Fee
	•	Receive counselor contact details
	•	Manage hired counselors list

Rapport does NOT facilitate therapy for organizations.
